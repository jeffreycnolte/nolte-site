## The challenge

Breeze needed a patient-facing dental platform that integrated with clinical workflows, handled protected health information under HIPAA, and launched within four months to meet investor commitments.

Most consultancies quoted six to nine months. Several couldn't commit to a fixed timeline at all.

## What we delivered

We scoped the engagement into 47 discrete deliveries, each with acceptance criteria and a fixed price. NolteOS forecasted completion at week 16 with 95% confidence.

The platform launched in week 15. On budget. To the dollar.

Key deliveries included:
- HIPAA-compliant patient data architecture
- Clinical workflow engine with real-time provider notifications
- Patient portal with appointment management
- Insurance verification integration
- Admin dashboard for practice management

## The partnership

Two years in, Breeze continues to ship with Nolte. The platform has scaled to multiple practices, and every engagement since launch has been forecasted and delivered predictably.

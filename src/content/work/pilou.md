## The challenge

Pilou needed a financial services platform that could operate under strict regulatory requirements while delivering a modern user experience. Every feature had to be built with compliance in mind from day one — not retrofitted after launch.

## What we delivered

We built Pilou's platform from concept to production using our predictive delivery model. Every delivery was scoped with regulatory requirements as first-class acceptance criteria, ensuring compliance wasn't an afterthought.

### Key deliveries

- **Regulatory-compliant data architecture** — Built to meet financial industry data handling standards with full audit trail from day one
- **Transaction processing pipeline** — Real-time processing with complete audit visibility across every state change
- **Compliance reporting dashboard** — Automated reporting that satisfies regulatory obligations without manual intervention
- **Identity verification flow** — KYC/AML integration built as discrete deliveries with testable acceptance criteria

## The result

Pilou launched on time with zero compliance gaps. Every delivery was forecasted by NolteOS and priced per delivery — no hourly billing, no scope surprises. The platform now handles thousands of transactions with full regulatory compliance.

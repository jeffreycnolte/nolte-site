## The challenge

Wake's founders had a vision for a mental wellness platform that served both employees and HR teams. They needed a mobile app (iOS and Android), an HR dashboard, and a content management system — all built from a pitch deck to a launched product.

As a pre-seed company, every dollar mattered. They couldn't afford a six-month discovery phase or an engagement that ran over budget.

## What we delivered

We started with product strategy — defining the core experience, user flows, and technical architecture. Then we scoped the build into deliveries, forecasted the timeline, and priced the engagement.

Key deliveries included:
- iOS and Android mobile apps (React Native)
- Guided wellness content player
- HR admin dashboard with team analytics
- Content management system for wellness programs
- Push notification and engagement engine
- Employee onboarding flow

## The outcome

Wake launched across all three platforms on schedule. The predictable delivery model gave the founding team confidence to fundraise against a live product with a clear roadmap — not a prototype with a prayer.

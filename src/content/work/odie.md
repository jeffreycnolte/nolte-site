## The challenge

Odie set out to build a modern pet insurance platform from scratch. The catch: carrier requirements and state-by-state regulatory obligations meant every feature had hard compliance deadlines that couldn't slip.

Filing dates are set by state regulators, not product teams. Missing one means losing the ability to operate in that state.

## What we delivered

We partnered with Odie from day zero — product strategy through Series A. The engagement was scoped into phased delivery plans, each forecasted by NolteOS and priced per delivery.

Key deliveries included:
- Multi-state compliant quoting engine
- Policy administration system
- Claims processing pipeline
- Agent portal and commission management
- Consumer-facing enrollment flow
- Regulatory filing documentation system

Every delivery met its compliance deadline. Every phase shipped within forecast.

## The outcome

Odie raised their Series A with a live, compliant platform operating across multiple states. The partnership has run for three years — every engagement forecasted, every delivery priced up front.

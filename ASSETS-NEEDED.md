## Image Guidelines

- **Format:** JPG for photos, PNG for screenshots with transparency, SVG for logos and icons
- **Size:** Hero images ~1200px wide, inline images ~800px wide, logos ~200px wide
- **Style:** Clean, minimal, on-brand. No stock photography. Real product screenshots preferred.
- **Tone:** Professional but human. The brand is Magician + Hero archetype вЂ” confident, clear, not corporate.
- **Colors:** Images should work against both `#fafaf8` (warm white) and `#000000` (dark) backgrounds.
